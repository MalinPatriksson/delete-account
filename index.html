Delete Account – Mini Memories

Last updated: 2025-08-17 • Developer: R&M Group AB • Contact: info@rmdesign.se

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open Mini Memories and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile picture.
  3. Select Delete profile and confirm.

What happens: Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately: albums, child profiles, events, photos, descriptions, and preferences. We do not retain a recoverable copy.

Delete specific data only (without deleting the account)

You can remove individual items directly in the app:
• Delete photos/events from the item menu
• Delete child profiles and albums from their respective menus

Alternative: Request by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from your registered address at info@rmdesign.se with the subject Delete Mini Memories account.
We’ll verify ownership and complete the deletion. Email requests are handled within 30 days.

Ta bort konto (svenska)

  1. Öppna Mini Memories och logga in.
  2. Tryck på din profilbild.
  3. Välj Ta bort profil och bekräfta.

Det som händer: Ditt konto och all kopplad data raderas omedelbart: album, barnprofiler, händelser, foton, texter och inställningar. Vi sparar ingen återställningsbar kopia.

Radera enskild data (utan att ta bort kontot)

Du kan ta bort enskilda saker direkt i appen:
• Ta bort foton/händelser via objektets meny
• Ta bort barnprofiler och album via respektive meny

Alternativ: Begäran via mejl

Om du inte kan nå appen, mejla från din registrerade adress till info@rmdesign.se med ämnet Ta bort Mini Memories-konto.
Vi verifierar ägarskap och slutför borttagningen inom 30 dagar.

Data retention

We delete account data immediately when deletion is triggered in-app. Email requests are processed within 30 days. Backups, if any, are overwritten as part of our normal rotation and aren’t used to restore deleted accounts.
